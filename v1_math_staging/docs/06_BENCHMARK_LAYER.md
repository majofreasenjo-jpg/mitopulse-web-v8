# Benchmark Layer

## Objetivo
Medir científicamente si el sistema realmente mejora.

## Qué evalúa
- ranking quality
- calibration quality
- temporal forecasting quality
- economic utility
- robustness under drift

## Por dominio
### Banking
- precision proxy
- loss avoided estimate
- alert usefulness

### Marketplace
- coordination discovery count
- payout risk ranking quality

### Energy
- instability anticipation lead time
- failure spread detection quality

### Logistics
- bottleneck anticipation lead time
- route disruption anticipation quality

## Entregables
- benchmark reports
- domain scorecards
- calibration drift report
- false positive / false negative cost tracking
